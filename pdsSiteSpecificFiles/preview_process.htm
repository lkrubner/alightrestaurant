PREVIEW

Your preview dinner is an opportunity to casually visit Parlor during a member dinner and experience the perks of the club. If you have specific questions about Parlor, our community and the club’s amenities, a membership director will be on hand to answer them.

APPLY

If you decide you would like to apply for membership, the club requires that you fill out an online application in order to provide Parlor with basic background information. Interviews may be arranged for the same night as your preview dinner.

INTERVIEW

After your interview, our membership committee will make a decision regarding your membership status within approximately 2 days. Upon acceptance, selected individuals are required pay the club’s annual dues of $1,500. Members will then receive a key to the club.
